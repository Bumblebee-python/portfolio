The chart demonstrates the Trend Joining Level (TJL), which is a crucial point used for entering a position within an ongoing trend. The TJL represents an optimal entry point for traders looking to join a trend, whether it be bullish or bearish, at a favorable price level. Often referred to as a "sniper entry," this strategy allows traders to enter with precision, maximizing the potential for profit while minimizing risk. The TJL is especially useful in trending markets, providing traders with a reliable point to capitalize on market momentum.
